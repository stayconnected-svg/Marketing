# KEY RULES

1. **Bryan's name never appears** in Emails 1–5. He is only introduced in the Warm Handoff.
2. **No performance guarantees** anywhere in the sequence.
3. **No aggressive or spammy language.** Every email should read as if a respected colleague wrote it.
4. **Unsubscribe link and physical address** on every email (CAN-SPAM).
5. **LPL compliance disclosures** only required on the Warm Handoff (where Bryan is named).
6. **A/B subject lines** should be tested with 50/50 splits; winning variant promoted after 100+ sends per version.
7. **Personalization tokens** must be populated before send: `[First Name]`, `[City]`, `[Practice Name]`, `[State]`.
8. **Stop the sequence immediately** if a prospect replies (positive or negative). Route positive replies to the Warm Handoff template. Route negative replies or unsubscribes to suppression list.
