# Sample Prospect List — HNW Business Owners, Northeast Ohio

**For:** Bryan McInerney
**Target:** High net worth business owners sensitive to taxes and fees, likely already have a wealth manager
**Geography:** Northeast Ohio (Akron, Cleveland, Canton, Hudson, Medina, Youngstown corridor)

---

## How We Find These People

We identify business owners through:
- Ohio Secretary of State filings (LLC/Corp registered agents who are also owners)
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator (title filters: Owner, CEO, President, Founder + NE Ohio + company size 10-200)
- Local business journals (Crain's Cleveland Business, Akron Beacon business section)
- Industry association directories (NE Ohio manufacturing, construction, professional services)
- Referral networks and COI databases

We target owners of businesses with $2M–$50M in annual revenue — large enough to generate high personal income, small enough that the owner IS the decision-maker on their own financial planning.

## Why These People Are Ideal for Bryan

Every person on this list shares these characteristics:

1. **High personal income** — likely $300K–$1M+ annually
2. **Probably already has a wealth manager** — but hasn't questioned fees in years
3. **Sensitive to taxes** — business ownership creates complex tax exposure
4. **Has a future exit event** — business sale, partner buyout, or succession plan
5. **Underserved by current advisor** — generic portfolio management, no insurance-based strategies, no real tax optimization
6. **NE Ohio based** — within Bryan's geography
