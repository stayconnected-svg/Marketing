## What We NEVER Say

We maintain a banned-word list based on FINRA advertising rules. These words never appear in any outreach:

| Category | Banned Terms |
|---|---|
| False promises | guaranteed, secure, safe, ensure, achieve |
| Superlatives | best, top, elite, superior, premier |
| Urgency/scarcity | don't miss out, act fast, limited time, opportunity of a lifetime |
| Misleading credibility | trusted, unbiased, objective, conflict-free, proven |
| Absolute market claims | always, never, will (as certainty about returns) |
| Product overselling | solution, solve, eliminate risk, maximize, optimize |
| Credential inflation | expert, guru, #1, award-winning |

## The Approval Process (One-Time)

1. We write 5-8 email templates + 2 LinkedIn messages
2. You review them (already done — see attached templates)
3. You submit the batch to LPL compliance for pre-approval
4. LPL approves (typical turnaround: days, not weeks for template batches)
5. We use those exact approved messages going forward — personalized with prospect names and cities, but the core language never changes

**After step 5, you never have to think about compliance again for these templates.**

## Automatic Protections We Handle

- **Unsubscribe requests:** Processed same-day, permanently suppressed
- **Do-not-contact list:** Maintained and checked before every send
- **CAN-SPAM compliance:** Physical address + unsubscribe on every email
- **Send volume limits:** Controlled pacing, never mass-blast behavior
- **Suppression on reply:** Sequence stops immediately when someone responds (positive or negative)
- **No securities content in prospecting:** Initial emails discuss fees and reviews only — never specific products, returns, or strategies
