The Problem With Most Annuity Ownership

Three issues I saw repeatedly in 20 years working inside insurance companies — and the reason I started this firm.

Complexity by Design

Annuity contracts are written to protect the insurance company, not to be understood by you. Most owners cannot explain what they own.

Hidden Fee Layers

Mortality charges, rider fees, sub-account expenses, surrender penalties — most owners are unaware of the total annual cost they're paying.

Missed Opportunities

Contracts change. Markets shift. Many owners are in positions that no longer serve them — but they don't know because nobody's shown them.
